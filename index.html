Goodbye Viking's Information
BIO

Once upon a time before the pandemic (2019), Tyler was just thinking to himself 'Gee, I'd like to be in a band! In fact - I'd like it to be a POP PUNK/SYNTH/SKA kind of band!'

So, he got to hurrying and started to gather all the things he would need to be the best POP PUNK/SYNTH/SKA BAND in ALL of Seattle!

He wrote some songs.

He got some gear.

And he made many magical friends along the way.

And Now - Flash Forward to Today: 2 Albums, 2 EPs, Several Singles/Comps, A Video Game Soundtrack, 2 West Coast Tours, 2 Appearances at FEST, over 100 shows and soon a Tour of Japan - GOODBYE VIKING has become one of the most must-see bands in the Pacific Northwest.

Influenced by 90s Ska Punk bands like NOFX, Rancid and Goldfinger along with a blend of 80's bands like Devo, Talking Heads and They Might Be Giants - GOODBYE VIKING has been taking their unique sound and brand of fun all over the Western United States for the last 5 years.

We'd love to come hang out and split a plate of veggie nachos with you while we talk about your feelings... and then melt faces on stage afterwards. Let us know!
