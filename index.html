Goodbye Viking's Information
BIO

Once upon a time before the pandemic (2019), Tyler was just thinking to himself 'Gee, I'd like to be in a band! In fact - I'd like it to be a POP PUNK/SYNTH/SKA kind of band!'

So, he got to hurrying and started to gather all the things he would need to be the best POP PUNK/SYNTH/SKA BAND in ALL of Seattle!

He wrote some songs.

He got some gear.

And he made many magical friends along the way.

And Now - Flash Forward to Today: 2 Albums, 3 EPs, Several Singles/Comps, A Video Game Soundtrack, 2 West Coast Tours, 2 Performances at FEST, 99.9 KISW Band of the Week, Loud N' Local Featured Band, over 100 shows and soon a Tour of Japan - GOODBYE VIKING has become one of the most CAN-see and hardest-working bands in the Pacific Northwest. (And you really should.)

Influenced by 90's/2000's punk bands like Green Day, Superchunk, Bomb The Music Industry!, Pain, Atom & His Package, The Aquabats, MU330, and Treephort, with 80's bands like They Might Be Giants, DEVO, and The Dead Milkmen - GOODBYE VIKING has been taking their unique sound and brand of fun all over the Western United States over the last 5 years.

With the release of their sophomore full length album 'PROBABLY', GOODBYE VIKING has developed a cleaner, more focused sound. The first single, 'IRONDALE' is a straight-forward ripper of a song that shows the bands growth in song writing, focus and execution.

GOODBYE VIKING is currently available for shows throughout the Pacific Northwestern Region and beyond. Contact us on Instagram - @GOODBYEVIKING - for more details and availability.
